What this is

A shared music queue for Uttarayan — paste a YouTube song link and it joins the line to play out loud, one song at a time, in the order it was added. No need to pass a phone around or fight over the aux cable: everyone can add a song from their own phone, see how long the wait is before their song plays, and skip to the next one if they're an admin. When nobody's added anything, a background playlist keeps the music going so there's never silence — and it steps aside the instant someone adds a real song.
